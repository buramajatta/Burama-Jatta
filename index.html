2022 World Cup: Brazil

Brazil World Cup 2022

The record 5 time World Cup champions will look for their 6th championship in Qatar. Brazil is in Group H and will play against Serbia, Switzerland, and Cameroon in the group stages.

Goalkeepers: Alisson, Ederson, Weverton Defenders: Marquinhos , Eder Militao, Thiago Silva (Captain), Danilo, Alex Telles, Dani Alves, Alex Sandro, Bremer

Midfielders: Fabinho, Fred, Casemiro, Lucas Paqueta, Bruno Guimaraes, Everton Ribiero

Forwards: Gabriel Martinelli, Neymar, Rodrygo, Vinicius Jr., Raphinha, Gabriel Jesus, Richarlison, Pedro

Neymar Jr. will be the star player for Brazil.

When it comes to the World Cup, Brazil are the kings. A footballing culture such as Brazil waits every 4 years to see their country lift the world famous trophy. Legends of the game are Brazilian and have all led their teams to glory. After heartbreak in 2014 and 2018, this experienced Brazil side will look to find World Cup glory.
